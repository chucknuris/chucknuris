### I turn real-world problems into software that lasts.

Most of my work lives behind the screens you use every day. I start from the actual problem — not the ticket — then build the simplest thing that holds up in production. Six years and 15+ shipped projects in, I've learned that the work that matters is **clear, disciplined, and built to last.**

Whether I'm owning a product end-to-end or dropping into an existing team, the mindset is the same — which is exactly why I slot into almost any team.

### About

- I solve real problems across the stack — from **street-level geospatial platforms used by millions** to full-stack fintech and government products.
- Today I focus on **distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and AI applied to real-world problems**.
- Equally at home **owning a product end-to-end** or **joining an existing team** — from architecture decisions through to shipped code.
- **M.Sc. Software Engineering & Internet Computing** @ TU Vienna · based in Vienna, Austria.
- Four languages — 🇧🇦 native · 🇩🇪 🇬🇧 fluent · 🇮🇹 basic.
- Off the keyboard: 3rd-dan black belt, chess, table tennis, football — same discipline, different arena.

### What I'm exploring now

Across a steady stream of client and product work, the thread I keep pulling on is **agentic AI** — systems where LLMs don't just answer, they take real actions. Right now that means going deep on **agentic workflows on Google Cloud** (Vertex AI Agents and the Google Agent Development Kit), and on agent tooling with **Claude** and **Gemini** — applied, as always, to real-world problems rather than demos.
